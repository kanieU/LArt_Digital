About L'Art

Nice to meet you, I'm Jaqueline Ambrósio. I have a degree in Photography and Graphic Design and a postgraduate degree in Marketing. In 2020, I founded Studio L'Art Digital, specializing in design, photography, and filmmaking in Americana. The team's mission is to bring the visual identity of brands and products to life,

creating designs that, beyond their functionality and aesthetics, can tell stories and captivate consumers. With dedication and expertise, we use the power of creativity in each brand and packaging, transforming ideas into images that communicate and inspire.
